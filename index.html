Product description

This unisex trucker jacket channels '80s aviator energy. The archival squared silhouette tapers from oversized shoulders to a slim, slightly cropped hem, and features a tiny vertical front pocket on the front.

Crafted in Italy from comfort denim in regenerative cotton, this dark blue DNA wash showcases precise abrasions and a natural lived-in fade. Developed on selvedge denim with a green cast, the wash references Diesel's archival "Turbo" finish. Refined details include a "Made in Italy" embroidery inside the waistband, a dark brown leather back patch, and a concave D-Eye button with a vintage patina - a blend of craftsmanship and heritage appeal.
